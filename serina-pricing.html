FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our pricing and how we work.

Do you offer payment plans? +
Yes! We offer flexible payment plans for all our services. Typically we require a 50% deposit to start the project and the remaining 50% upon completion. For larger projects we can split payments into 3 milestones. Contact us to discuss what works best for you.
Are these prices fixed or negotiable? +
The prices shown are starting points. Final pricing depends on your specific requirements, timeline and project complexity. We always provide a detailed quote before any work begins — no surprises. Book a free consultation and we'll give you an accurate estimate within 24 hours.
What is included in the free consultation? +
Our free 30-minute consultation covers understanding your business goals, discussing your project requirements, recommending the best solution for your budget, and providing a rough timeline and cost estimate. There is absolutely no obligation to proceed after the consultation.
Do you offer ongoing maintenance after project completion? +
Yes. We offer monthly maintenance packages starting from KES 5,000/month that cover security updates, content updates, performance monitoring and technical support. Enterprise packages include maintenance for the first 3-12 months at no extra cost.
How long does a typical project take? +
A basic website takes 1-2 weeks. A business website takes 3-4 weeks. A custom web application or mobile app typically takes 6-12 weeks depending on complexity. We always agree on a timeline before starting and keep you updated throughout the process.
Do the prices include hosting and domain? +
Our website prices do not include hosting and domain costs as these vary by provider. However we can recommend affordable hosting options and help you set everything up. Domain registration in Kenya typically costs KES 1,000-3,000 per year and hosting from KES 3,000-8,000 per year.
