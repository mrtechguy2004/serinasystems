Sheet 02/06Origin

Why I Started Building

A short log on where Serina Systems came from, and what keeps me building.

I founded Serina Systems with one belief: world-class digital solutions shouldn't be out of reach for businesses across Kenya and beyond. Growing up, I saw the gap between innovation and opportunity on the continent — strong ideas without easy access to the technology and talent to bring them to life. I set out to close that gap.

The company name carries the mission. SERINA stands for Systems, Engineering, Research, Intelligence, Networking, and Advancement — the principles I hold every project to.

I started small in Nakuru with a tight team and a simple goal: deliver software that competes globally while staying rooted in the realities of the African market. Less than a year in, that's grown into a digital transformation partner working across Nakuru, Laikipia, and Nairobi.
