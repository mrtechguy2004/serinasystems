Our Story

Building the Future, One Line of Code at a Time

Serina Systems was born from a simple yet powerful belief: technology should empower businesses, not complicate them.

Where It All Began

We thought, what if we could have a system that is engineered with precision, fueled by research, and guided by intelligence? A system that thrives on networking and never stops advancing. That vision became SERINA — Systems, Engineering, Research, Intelligence, Networking, and Advancement — the essence of how we build smarter, connected, future-ready solutions.

Founded in Nakuru, Kenya, Serina Systems emerged from the vision of passionate technologists who saw the untapped potential of African businesses in the digital age. We started with a small team of dedicated developers and a mission to deliver world-class software solutions that could compete on the global stage.

From a local software venture, we’ve grown into a digital transformation partner serving clients across Laikipia, Nakuru, and Nairobi. Fueled by innovation, driven by results, and guided by excellence, we craft solutions built for growth.

Today, we stand as a testament to what's possible when cutting-edge technology meets African ingenuity and global ambition.

Meet our Founder and CEO

A Visionary Rooted in Purpose,
Driven by Impact

Fidel Ouma is a passionate technologist and entrepreneur who built Serina Systems on a bold dream — to make world-class digital solutions accessible to businesses across Africa and beyond. Growing up witnessing the gap between innovation and opportunity in the continent, Fidel was determined to bridge it.

With a background spanning software engineering, product strategy, and business development, Fidel has steered Serina Systems from a lean startup in Nairobi to a full-service digital transformation partner serving clients across multiple continents. His leadership philosophy centres on excellence, empathy, and the relentless belief that African ingenuity can compete on the global stage.

Fidel's dream is simple yet powerful: a future where every ambitious African business has access to the technology and talent it needs to thrive — and Serina Systems is the engine driving that future forward.
