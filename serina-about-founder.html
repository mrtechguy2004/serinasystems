My Story

Why I Started Serina Systems

A journey rooted in purpose, driven by the belief that great technology should be within reach of every ambitious business.

Where It All Began

I founded Serina Systems with the vision of making innovative and affordable digital solutions accessible to businesses across Kenya and beyond. Growing up, I saw the gap between innovation and opportunity on the continent — talented businesses with big ideas, but without easy access to the technology and talent needed to bring them to life. I was determined to bridge that gap.

The name itself carries the mission: SERINA stands for Systems, Engineering, Research, Intelligence, Networking, and Advancement — the principles I wanted every project we touch to embody.

Starting in Nakuru with a small, dedicated team, I set out to build a company that could deliver world-class software solutions while staying rooted in the realities and opportunities of the African market.

Today, that vision has grown into a digital transformation partner serving clients across Laikipia, Nakuru, and Nairobi — and I'm just getting started.
