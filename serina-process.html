FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with Serina Systems.

How long does a typical project take?
▾
Most MVP projects are delivered in 8–12 weeks. The exact timeline depends on scope and complexity. After the discovery phase, we provide a detailed project plan with milestones, and we stick to it. Larger enterprise projects may take 3–6 months, broken into clear phases with deliverables at each stage.
How do you ensure website visibility within a short period of time?
▾
"Great question. Website visibility doesn't happen overnight, but with the right strategy, you can see meaningful traction within 30–90 days. Here's how we approach it: First, we prioritize quick-win SEO. We audit your site immediately — fixing technical issues like page speed, mobile responsiveness, broken links, and meta tags. These are low-hanging fruit that search engines reward fast. Second, we set up and optimize Google Business Profile and local listings if you're targeting a local audience. This alone can get you appearing in map results within days. Third, we run targeted paid ads (Google/Meta) in parallel. While organic SEO builds momentum, paid search puts you in front of the right audience immediately — day one visibility while organic catches up. Fourth, we build content with search intent in mind. We research exactly what your audience is searching for and create optimized landing pages and blog content that answers those queries directly. Fifth, we focus on backlink building. We secure mentions and links from credible, relevant websites — one of the strongest signals search engines use to rank sites. Finally, we track everything. Google Search Console, Analytics, and rank tracking tools give us weekly data so we can double down on what's working and pivot quickly on what isn't. The honest truth is: SEO is a 3–6 month game for sustainable results, but paid ads and technical fixes deliver visibility within the first few weeks. We combine both so you're never waiting and never wasting budget."
What happens if the requirements change during development?
▾
Change is expected in software development — that's why we use agile methodology. Minor scope adjustments are handled within sprints. For significant changes, we do a quick impact assessment (timeline, cost, complexity) and present options. We never proceed with major changes without your explicit approval and a signed change order.
Do you provide post-launch support?
▾
Absolutely. We offer flexible post-launch support packages — from a basic bug-fix retainer to a full dedicated team for ongoing feature development. We also provide a 30-day free warranty period after launch where we fix any defects at no charge. Long-term partnerships are our preferred model.
Who owns the source code and intellectual property?
▾
You do — 100%. Upon final payment, all source code, assets, and intellectual property are fully transferred to you. We provide complete handover documentation, repository access, and a knowledge transfer session so your team can independently manage and evolve the product.
How do I get started?
▾
It starts with a free 30-minute discovery call. We'll listen to your idea, ask the right questions, and give you an honest assessment of feasibility, timeline, and rough cost — no strings attached. From there, if it's a fit, we'll send you a detailed proposal within 48 hours. Click "Get a Quote" to book your call today.
