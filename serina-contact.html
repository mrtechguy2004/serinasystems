Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the questions we get most often. Don't see yours? Just ask.

How long does it take to build a mobile app?
+
Timelines vary by complexity. A basic MVP typically takes 6–10 weeks, while a full-featured application can take 3–6 months. During our discovery call, we'll scope your project and give you a realistic timeline with milestones.
Do you work with international clients?
+
Absolutely. We work with clients across 16 countries including the USA, UK, UAE, Sweden, Canada, Australia, and across Africa. We accommodate different time zones and use collaboration tools like Slack, Notion, and Jira to keep everyone aligned.
What does the development process look like?
+
We follow an agile approach: Discovery & Define → Design & Prototype → Build & Test → Launch & Support. You'll receive bi-weekly demos and progress reports so you're always in the loop. No black-box development here.
How much does a software project cost?
+
Costs depend on scope, complexity, and technology. A simple website starts around KES 20,000, while complex enterprise systems can run several million. We provide a detailed quote after a free initial consultation — no surprises.
Do you offer post-launch support and maintenance?
+
Yes. All projects include a 30-day free post-launch support period. We also offer ongoing maintenance retainers covering bug fixes, performance monitoring, feature updates, and security patches.
Can you work on my existing project or codebase?
+
Yes. We regularly take over existing projects for redesigns, feature additions, performance improvements, or bug fixes. We'll audit your current codebase and provide an honest assessment before starting any work.
